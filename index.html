About the University

The university of Uyo was founded in 1991 by the Federal Government of Nigeria. Formerly, the University was known as the University of Cross River state which was established in 1981 by the then Cross River State Government. The university was jointly owned by Akwa ibom and Cross River State Governments. What is known today as the University of Uyo has a humble but historical evolution. In 1991, the erstwhile University of Cross River State assumed a new status and nomenclature called the University of Uyo, a Federal University. It is a citadel of learning, research and community development.
